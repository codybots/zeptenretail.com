01 Who We Are

Zepten Retail ("we", "us", or "our") is an online marketplace seller operating on Amazon India, Flipkart, Meesho, and other platforms. We operate an internal business management platform to run our seller operations.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information in connection with our operations and internal software systems.

02 Information We Collect

We collect information through our internal platform in the following categories:

  • Order information: Order IDs, fulfilment status, product details, shipping addresses, and delivery tracking data for orders placed on marketplace platforms.
  • Inventory data: Stock levels, inbound shipment details, and warehouse inventory records.
  • Financial data: Sales reports, account settlements, and transaction records from marketplace platforms.
  • Account data: Seller account performance metrics and listing data.
  • System access logs: IP address, device type, browser, login timestamps, and actions taken within our internal platform, for security and audit purposes.

03 How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for internal business operations:

  • Processing and fulfilling customer orders across FBA, Easy Ship, and self-ship channels
  • Managing product listings, pricing, and inventory across marketplaces
  • Generating sales reports and business performance analytics
  • Booking shipments with courier partners for self-ship orders
  • Maintaining audit logs for system security and access control
  • Informing advertising decisions on Amazon and other platforms

We do not use Amazon buyer information for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond fulfilling the order for which it was provided.

04 Amazon Selling Partner API Data

Our internal platform accesses Amazon seller data through the Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API). This section describes specifically how that data is handled.

Data accessed via SP-API includes: Order details, buyer shipping addresses, inventory levels, financial reports, listing data, and account performance metrics.
  • Access scope: SP-API data is accessed exclusively for Zepten Retail's own seller account. We do not build applications for or share access with other sellers.
  • Storage: Amazon data is stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database on a private server. All personally identifiable information (PII) fields are encrypted at the application layer using AES-256-GCM before storage.
  • Retention: Order PII (buyer name, address) is anonymised 30 days after order delivery. Anonymised sales and inventory data may be retained for analytics purposes. Financial records are retained as required by applicable law.
  • Access control: Amazon data is accessible only to authorised Zepten Retail personnel based on their job function. Access is enforced via role-based controls in our identity system.
  • No resale or redistribution: Amazon Information is never sold, licensed, or distributed to any third party for any purpose.

05 Data Sharing

We share Amazon Information in the following limited circumstance only:

  • Courier partners (self-ship orders only): For orders fulfilled via self-ship, we share the buyer's name, delivery address, and order ID with Amazon-whitelisted courier companies solely for the purpose of booking and completing delivery. This data is transmitted at the time of order booking and is not retained by us beyond the shipment lifecycle.

We do not share Amazon buyer information with any advertising networks, analytics providers, data brokers, or other third parties.

06 Data Retention

  • Order PII (buyer name, address, contact) is anonymised 30 days after confirmed delivery.
  • Anonymised order and sales data is retained for internal analytics and advertising performance measurement.
  • System access logs are retained for 90 days.
  • Financial records are retained for the period required under applicable Indian tax and accounting regulations.
  • Database backups are encrypted and automatically deleted after 30 days.

07 Security

We implement the following technical and organisational controls to protect Amazon and customer data:

  • Network security: Server firewall restricts inbound access to HTTPS (443) and SSH (22, key-based only). Databases are on a private internal network with no public exposure.
  • Encryption in transit: All data is transmitted over TLS 1.3. Internal services communicate over an encrypted network.
  • Encryption at rest: Data is stored on AES-256 encrypted volumes. PII fields are additionally encrypted at the application layer.
  • Access control: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all system access. Role-based permissions limit data access to job-relevant functions only.
  • Credential security: API credentials are stored as encrypted environment variables, never in source code. Secrets are rotated quarterly.
  • Audit logging: All system access events are written to an append-only audit log capturing timestamp, user ID, IP address, and action taken.
  • Incident response: We maintain a documented incident response plan. In the event of a data breach involving Amazon Information, we will notify Amazon at security@amazon.com within 24 hours of detection.

08 Your Rights

If you are an Amazon buyer whose order information we have processed as part of fulfilling your order, you may contact us to:

  • Request confirmation of what data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)

Please contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

09 Cookies

Our internal platform uses session cookies strictly for authentication and security purposes. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics on any platform operated by Zepten Retail.
